Melissa Marie Benoist

Melissa Marie Benoist (/bəˈnɔɪst/; French: [bənwa]; born October 4, 1988)[2][3][4] is an American actress and singer. She has performed on stage and in television and film. She is known for her portrayal of the titular character in the CBS/CW DC Comics–based superhero drama series Supergirl (2015–present).Benoist rose to prominence for her portrayal of Marley Rose on the Fox musical comedy-drama television series Glee (2012–2014).
